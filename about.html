Hi! I'm a full-stack javascript developer with a nontraditional background. Always curious (and a touch precocious), I graduated from Wright State University with a BA in French and Philosophy in 2016. I was 20 years old, out of school, and directionless. Without papers to write or complex philisophical arguments to disect, I found myself overwhelmingly bored. I craved a challenge.

Thanks to the suggestion of friends, I began exploring codecademy and freecodecamp on my days off. I soon found that my language and logic skills translated easily to the world of software and web development -- and the field offers an endless stream of puzzles for me to solve. I went from newbie to coding-addict in a matter of weeks.

With the help of open-source resources and the Bloc web-developer program, I learned HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Angular.js, Ruby on Rails, and more. Most importantly, I learned how to learn: I'm a master Google-er, and always looking to learn as much as I can.
